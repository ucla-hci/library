### Defining hypothesis generation
> We thus define hypothesis generation as generating natural language theories/explanations about observed phenomena.

The definition here seems to exclusively focus on explanation of observed phenomena and might not apply to discovery? The paper does seem to realize this---

> Existing work ... tends to conflate explanatory power with novelty for hypothesis generation ebcause it is natural for scientists to expect hypothese to contribute to scientific advances ...

Historically, logical positivism matched this paper's interpretation of hypothesis generation whereas latter schools of thoughts also considered creative discovery as well.

The formal definition in §2 feels like "finding latent causes".
