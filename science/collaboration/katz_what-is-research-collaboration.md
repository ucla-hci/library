### Collaboration has a fuzzy boundary
Some collaboration is involved (all contribute to all activities) while others is loosely coupled (each contributes to smaller, modular components)
> A research collaboration therefore has a very ‘fuzzy’ or ill-defined border. Exactly where that border is drawn is a matter of social convention and is open to negotiation.
